About this Project

In the last few years european cities have shown important changes in term of mobility. One of the most important factors in these changes is linked to the birth and grownth of many carsharing services.
Visualizing and analyzing the data coming from these services can allow us to better understand how the city is used and discover hidden mobility patterns.
In february 2014 we decided to collect data from one of the main carsharing services in Milan called Enjoy.
